# Mahatma Kawa

**Machine Learning Engineer** at [Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI)](https://bri.co.id) | Building AI systems for one of Southeast Asia's largest banks

---

### About

I design and deploy machine learning systems that solve real problems in banking — from LLM-powered customer interactions to ML pipelines that operate at institutional scale. My work sits at the intersection of applied NLP, large language models, and production ML engineering.

I'm an early adopter by nature — I stay on the bleeding edge of AI tooling and integrate new capabilities into my workflow the moment they prove useful. From high-performance LLM serving with vLLM and SGLang to AI-assisted development with Claude Code (skills, plugins, MCP servers, and agentic workflows), I treat every emerging tool as an opportunity to compound my engineering output.

Before BRI, I worked as an IoT Engineer at Telkom Indonesia and studied at Gadjah Mada University, where I explored robotics, computer vision, and embedded systems. That breadth informs how I approach ML — I think in systems, not just models.

### What I'm Working On

- Developing LLM applications for enterprise banking use cases
- Building retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems and AI agents
- Optimizing LLM inference and serving at scale (vLLM, SGLang, TensorRT-LLM)
- Leveraging agentic AI workflows — Claude Code skills/plugins, MCP integrations, and vibe coding to accelerate development
- Exploring speech AI and multimodal model integration
- Open-sourcing tools and patterns from my applied ML work
